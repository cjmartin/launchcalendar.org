SpaceX is set to launch Starlink Group 12-15 at 03:58 UTC on May 20, 2025, from SLC-40 at Cape Canaveral SFS, Florida. The Falcon 9 will carry 23 Starlink V2 Mini satellites, including 13 equipped with Direct to Cell capabilities, to low Earth orbit. The booster will attempt a landing on the droneship 'Just Read The Instructions.' The launch window is just 35 minutes long. This mission marks SpaceX's 60th Falcon 9 launch of the year.
