SpaceX plans to launch Starlink Group 12-22 with v2 Mini and Direct-to-Cell satellites from SLC-40, Cape Canaveral, on May 24, 2025 at 17:19 UTC. Booster landing is planned for one of SpaceX's Atlantic droneships. This mission will feature a mixture of satellite types launched to LEO on a southeasterly trajectory.
