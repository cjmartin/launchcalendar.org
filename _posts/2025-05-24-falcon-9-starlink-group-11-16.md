SpaceX has rescheduled the Starlink Group 11-16 launch to Friday, May 23 (local PDT date, May 24 UTC) at 03:19 UTC, following a delay to optimize launch conditions. A Falcon 9 with booster B1075, aiming for its 18th flight, will deploy 27 Starlink v2 Mini satellites to expand the constellation's polar shell from SLC-4E, Vandenberg SFB, California. Landing is planned on the droneship 'Of Course I Still Love You' (OCISLY) in the Pacific. Weather is favorable and live coverage will be available on SpaceX's X (formerly Twitter) and X TV.
